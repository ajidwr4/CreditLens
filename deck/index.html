// The Problem

DeFi lending is broken
for most people.

🔒

Over-Collateralization

You must lock more than you borrow. Capital is trapped, not deployed.

📭

No Credit History On-Chain

Reliable borrowers have no way to prove their repayment track record.

📊

No Risk Signal for Lenders

Lenders cannot assess borrower risk without collateral as a fallback.

🌍

Real-World Credit is Siloed

Mortgage, business loan, personal credit history — invisible on-chain.

// The Solution

Build credit identity
on-chain.

CreditLens creates a transparent, on-chain credit identity for every borrower — combining blockchain loan history with real-world credit records.

The higher your score, the better loan terms you can access. No collateral required.

Lenders get a reliable risk signal. Borrowers get rewarded for good behavior.

// How It Works — Mechanism A

Borrower-initiated
lending.

Post Request
→
Lenders Fund Offers
→
Borrower Accepts
→
Loan Created
→
Repay / Default

BORROWER

  • Post request with amount & expiry
  • View incoming offers with APR & duration
  • Accept the best offer
  • Receive tCTC, repay before deadline

LENDER

  • Browse open requests + credit scores
  • Fund offer with custom APR & duration
  • tCTC locked until accept / reject / expire
  • Refund available if not selected
05 / 10

// How It Works — Mechanism B & C

Public offers &
loan lifecycle.

Mechanism B

Public Offer

  • Lender posts offer with min credit score
  • Only qualifying borrowers can take it
  • Score verified on-chain at transaction time
  • First come, first served

Mechanism C

Loan Lifecycle

  • Active loan tracked on-chain
  • Repay before deadline → score +points
  • Miss deadline → anyone can markDefault
  • Default → score −points

Real World Credit

Record Minting

  • Issuer mints credit record on-chain
  • KPR, business loan, personal credit
  • Contributes to real world score
  • Publicly verifiable on Credit Records page
